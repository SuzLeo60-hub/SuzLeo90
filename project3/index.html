The Story of Coldplay

Coldplay, an alternative rock band officially formed in 1998, is led by Jonny Buckland, Chris Martin, Will Champion, and Guy Berryman. Originally a duo in 1990, the band formed was formed by Martin and Buckland in 1996 while the two men attended university in London. Shortly after Guy Berryman, a bassist, joined Pectoralz, the band became named Starfish. After will Champion joined, the group became known as Coldplay. The members of the band come from all over the United Kingdom. Coldplay's studio, called the Bakery, is located in North London. Coldplay's first music release, called "Safety," was released in 1998.
